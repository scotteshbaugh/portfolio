You and the City

Objective

To develop the skills to define and execute self-initiated work.

Statement

Austin is known for its unique character and amicable relationship to its natural surroundings. But in recent years, exploding growth threatens the city’s identity while an outdated zoning code baffles most residents. My focus was to help Austinites understand how zoning intimately affects their lives and lower the entry barriers to the conversation about the future of their communities.
