Glow Slow

Objective

To design a device that measures a specific task and challenges the preconceived notions of what a measuring device can be.

Statement

Foundational to the American culture is efficiency and progress. While this leads many Americans to achieve great goals, it has its drawbacks—especially when it comes to our diets. Eating quickly leads to an array of health issues including obesity, diabetes, and acid reflex, and also robs people of a simple pleasure in life.

The plate’s design interactions are subtle and minimal to manage a person’s eating habits without intrusions or restrictions. While most information technology places an emphasis on efficiency and goals, the plate avoids such pursuits to frame eating as an art form rather than a micromanaged task to complete.

Sensor Plates

The measuring plate sits beneath various dishes and detect the changes in weight as you eat using sensing technology.

Measuring Technology

The strain gauge load cell measures food by converting force into an electrical signal. The microprocessor computates the signal data to determine the correct color for LED lights.
