Quad Energy Challenge

An awarded project by the Green Fee Committee co-led by Riley Triggs of LZTArchitects.

Question

To design and operate an energy savings competition and educate residents about energy saving practices.

Context

My team was responsible for the courtyard installation, website, branding, and social media for the dorm-to-dorm part of the competition. My specific responsibility was to create an information graphic, work with a partner to design the website, and offer insight and labor to other team members developing the branding and courtyard installations.

One challenge was to create a consistent visual language throughout the digital and print media as well as the courtyard installation to cohesively bring the competition together. A second challenge was to establish how and what kind of information to display. The information needed to be both meaningful and clear to help residents make conclusions about their progress in the competition.
