In our time, we’ve created unmatched amounts of data and information but often fall short of creating knowledge. My aim bridges these gaps by making the complex clear. I am an advocate of technology but also a critic. It can neither cure nor poison our human nature but only amplify it. Reverent of this, my interest is at the intersection of technology and culture.
