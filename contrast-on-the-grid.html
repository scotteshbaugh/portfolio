Contrast on the Grid

From Syntax in Visual Communication taught by David Shields

Objective

To create a range of typographic compositions with modular grid structures for engaging and legible reading.

Context

This formal exercise was to create posters using a body of copy promoting the award ceremony for the Pritzker Architecture Prize.

With over a hundred iterations, practice and typographic principles such as hierarchy, grid structures, negative space, visual continuity, and text-image relationships distilled my work.
