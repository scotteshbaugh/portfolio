Contrast on the Grid

From Syntax in Visual Communication taught by David Shields

Objective

To explore the possibilities of typographic composition with modular grid structures.

Statement

With over a hundred iterations, practice and instruction of compositional and typographic principles distilled my work. Towards the beginning I practiced using negative space to create hierarchy. As form and varying typeface weights broadened my possibilities, I learned the value of creating visual continuity with the text and using it to reinforce the grid. The last step of the assignment allowed me to apply these principles to text-image relationships. This led to a framework of leading and disrupting the viewer for specific purposes to create legible and engaging reading.
